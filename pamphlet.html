Led by software engineering professionals with graduation from IIT Bombay in Computer Science and 15+ years of experience at Walmart Labs, Oracle, Amazon, VMware, and Broadcom. AI Agents are part of their daily work — this workshop comes from practical, hands-on experience, not theory.
